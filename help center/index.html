FAQs

How can I order?

You can order easily using our online platform. When you find a product you need, you can add it to cart, login and go through the ordering process. After the order is ready, you will receive order summary to your email. Order summary will also be stored to your account.

What information should I input when ordering?

our online ordering system will ask for all the important information you should submit. If you have a VAT number, please remember to submit it. This will make sure the shipment is not delayed because of the lack of VAT number

What payment methods can I use?

You can use all the major credit cards. If you are a customer with established customer relationship with HyTest Ltd. you are able to use invoice as a payment method on our online shopping checkout process. If invoicing option is not activated for you although you are repeated customer, please contact us.

How can I change delivery address?

Sign in to your account and go to “my account”. On “my account” you can change all your contact information.

Can I pay with invoice?

If you have already established customer relationship with HyTest, you will be granted ability to pay using invoice. If you have ordered several times from HyTest but you don't see the invoice as a paying option on the checkout, please contact us and we will check why the invoice is not activated on your account.

How to contact customer service?

If you have question regarding our online store (ordering, account questions, technical questions), please contact us On pricing and shipping related issues you can contact customer support at (web name)@helwan.com or call +028-2-512-0900.
